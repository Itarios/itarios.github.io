About me

I'm a MsC in Mathematics. I did my MsC in CIMAT (Centro de Investigación en Matemáticas), and my BS in mathematics at the University of Guanajuato.

I'm interested in Category Theory, Homotopy Type Theory, Commutative Algebra and Topology.
